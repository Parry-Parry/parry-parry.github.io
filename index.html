I am a 1st year PhD Candidate within the Information Retrieval group at the University of Glasgow under Dr Debasis Ganguly & Dr Sean MacAvaney. My work lies within adversarial robustness and efficient training of models. My interests mainly lie within Stochastic Processes and Information Retrieval.
Previously I completed my undergraduate degree in Computing Science with specialization in Data Management, my main focus was in Sequence to sequence learning applied to code translation. I have enjoyed both Graphic Design and teaching in the past. I provided graphics for DigitalSkillsEducation Scotland and Edinburgh University. From its inception, I was the longest-attending member of CoderDojo Scotland (for 5 years). I then voluntarily and professionally taught Computing Science in disadvantaged areas.
